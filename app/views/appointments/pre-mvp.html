Clashes and schedules

In order to schedule their appointment at the right time, the user needs to know what attendees already have scheduled. We see that there are two potential approaches which could help the user make a decision.

Prisoner schedules - what the attendees already have scheduled for a chosen date only:

  • Design in test v11 - individual - choose date, time and location upfront, show schedules for whole day (cards)
  • Design in test v11 - bulk - choose date, time and location upfront, show schedules for whole day (cards)
  • Design in test v11 - group - no events scheduled
  • Design in test v11 - individual - no events scheduled

DPS Homepage

This details a proposal to combine certain journeys in the service to aid with understanding the difference between a bulk and a group appointment.

This has been a long-running issues where different variants of content have failed to make this concept easier to understand for users.

Combining individual and group would be relatively simple in the back-end as an individual appt is essentially group but with one participate. For merging group and bulk, we'd need an additional branching page. We also have the option to keep all tiles as-is.

  • Variant 1 - one-to-one, set and group
  • Variant 2 - one-to-one, for a group of people
  • Variant 3 - schedule an appointment, schedule a set of appointments
  • DPS tiles ideation - Miro
  • DPS tiles - UR findings w/c 12 June 2023
