Clashes and schedules

In order to schedule their appointment at the right time, the user needs to know what attendees already have scheduled. We see that there are two potential approaches which could help the user make a decision:

  • prisoner schedules - what the attendees already have scheduled for a chosen date only
    • Design in test v11 - group - choose date, time and location upfront, show schedules for whole day (cards)
    • Design in test v11 - individual - choose date, time and location upfront, show schedules for whole day (cards)
    • Design in test v11 - bulk - choose date, time and location upfront, show schedules for whole day (cards)
    • Design in test v11 - group - no events scheduled
    • Design in test v11 - individual - no events scheduled
    • v10 - group - repeating - choose date, time and location upfront, show schedules for whole day (condensed)
    • v9 - group - choose date, time and location upfront, show schedules for whole day (condensed)
    • v8 - group - choose date and location upfront, time after (condensed)
    • v7 - group - choose date upfront, time after (condensed)
    • v6 - group - choose date and time upfront (condensed)
    • v5 - group - choose date and time upfront (tabular)
    • v4 - group - duplicates search results in 'find' but filters results as as you choose time and location
    • v3 - group - duplicates search results in 'find'
    • v2 - group - surfacing time periods (am, pm , ed)
    • v1 - individual - weekly schedule (similar to prisoner profile)

  • clashes - showing where there is a clash between a chosen date and time and what the attendees already have scheduled
