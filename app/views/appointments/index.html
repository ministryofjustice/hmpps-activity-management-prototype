Appointment creation

Capturing Future Regime Design (FRD) purposeful activity MVP

FRD have requested that we capture imforation on purposeful activity in our service. Previously some of this information was captured on NOMIS when creating activities, but it is new for appointments.

There are three options an appointment can fit into: Tier 1, Tier 2 and foundational tier (non-purposeful activity, including not in work).

For Tier 2 appointments, we must also capture who is providing/running this appointment.

Schedule an appointment

  • What is the appointment tier? new
  • Who is providing this appointment? new
  • Check your answers updated

Schedule a set of back-to-back appointments

  • What is the appointment tier? new
  • Who is providing these appointments? new
  • Check your answers updated

Alerts MVP+

From out options and risks analysis, we agreed an initial approach for surfacing alerts in appointments. This is as follows:

  • We will surface all alerts (by alert name) on a dedicated page
  • Only users who have alerts would be shown
  • Alerts which have a corresponding flag on the prisoner profile will be displayed as flags to remain consistent across DPS
  • All alerts (including ones with flags) are surfaced in a progressive disclosure

Schedule an appointment

  • alerts page - multiple people in the list new
  • all people with alerts removed - some attendees remain new
  • all people with alerts removed - no attendees remain no change

Schedule a set of back-to-backs

  • alerts page - multiple people in the list new
  • all people with alerts removed - one person on the list new
  • all people with alerts removed - some attendees remain new
  • all people with alerts removed - no attendees remain no change
