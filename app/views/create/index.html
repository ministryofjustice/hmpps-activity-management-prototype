Capturing Future Regime Design (FRD) purposeful activity MVP

FRD have requested that we capture imforation on purposeful activity in our service. Previously some of this information was captured on NOMIS when creating activities, but it is new for appointments.

There are three options an appointment can fit into: Tier 1, Tier 2 and foundational tier (non-purposeful activity, including not in work).

For Tier 2 appointments, we must also capture who is providing/running this activity.
